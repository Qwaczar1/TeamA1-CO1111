Treasure Hunt

The Treasure Hunt API aims to engage participants by integrating a treasure hunt with challenges, requiring players to collect information at specific locations. The API provides fake scores and questions to evaluate the app's aspects. Players must use their strategy, skills, and problem-solving abilities to win challenges. Exploration is required to unlock new locations and environments. Questions enhance knowledge and skills, with correct responses gaining points and skipped questions deducting points.
